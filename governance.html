Governance & Integrity

Pendleton Chapel Seminary is an independent religious institution rooted in Baptist tradition. Governance exists to protect integrity, transparency, and accountability.

Institutional Commitments

Local-Church Authority

Ordination and commissioning are governed by local churches.

Ethics & Care Standards

We form leaders committed to ethical ministry, consent, and non-coercive spiritual care.

Transparency

Program descriptions and outcomes are stated plainly; accreditation is stated explicitly if applicable.

Affiliation Note

Affiliation reflects shared heritage and cooperation; Pendleton Chapel Seminary is not governed by the Southern Baptist Convention or the Baptist General Convention of Texas.
