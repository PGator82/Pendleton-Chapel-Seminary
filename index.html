Pendleton Chapel Seminary

Discipleship • Chaplaincy • Formation

Pendleton Chapel Seminary forms disciples, pastors, and chaplains for faithful service in churches, communities, and public spaces. Rooted in a long-standing Southern Baptist tradition and shaped by chaplaincy practice, we prepare leaders to serve with peace, integrity, and wisdom.

Disciples Join Now

Identity

Calling, character, ethics, and spiritual maturity for sustainable ministry.

Formation

Spiritual disciplines, emotional resilience, prayer, and Scripture.

Mission

Gospel-centered service, reconciliation, and non-coercive witness.

Our Goals

Competent Spiritual Care

Graduates trained in pastoral presence, crisis care, and ethical ministry.

Church Strengthening

Leaders equipped to shepherd, teach, and serve local congregations.

Chaplaincy Readiness

Preparation for hospital, prison, hospice, campus, and community chaplaincy.

Peacemaking Ministry

Formation shaped by peace, blessing, and service.

Affiliation

Pendleton Chapel Seminary maintains longstanding connections within Southern Baptist life and the Baptist General Convention of Texas. Ordination and commissioning remain under local-church authority.

The Order of Melchizedek (Biblical Pattern)

Peace

Ministry that stabilizes chaos through presence and reconciliation.

Blessing

Service offered without coercion, honoring dignity and conscience.

Nourishment

Grounding care, prayer when invited, and meaningful presence.

Christ at the Center

Christ alone is our eternal High Priest; we affirm the priesthood of all believers.
