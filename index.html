Affiliation reflects shared heritage and cooperation; Pendleton Chapel Seminary is not governed by the Southern Baptist Convention or the Baptist General Convention of Texas.

Pendleton Chapel Seminary forms disciples, pastors, and chaplains for faithful service in churches, communities, and public spaces. Rooted in Baptist tradition and shaped by chaplaincy practice, we prepare leaders to serve with peace, integrity, and wisdom.
